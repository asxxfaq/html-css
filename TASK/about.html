About FC Barcelona

Founded in 1899, Futbol Club Barcelona is more than just a football club — it is a symbol of Catalan culture, pride, and passion. Known worldwide by the motto “Més que un club” (More than a club), Barça has a rich history filled with legendary players and unforgettable moments.

The club plays its home matches at the iconic Spotify Camp Nou stadium and has won numerous La Liga, Champions League, and domestic titles. Beyond football, FC Barcelona represents values of teamwork, respect, and community.
